Our Services

  • Dentures

    Custom-made full and hybrid dentures, mouth guards, sleep apnea equipment, and more.

  • Crowns

    Fully encase the entire visible portion of a tooth that lies at and above the gum line.

  • Bridges

    Pontics made from gold, alloys, porcelain, or a combination of these materials.

  • Herbst Appliance

    Dental mechanism that is usually used on children to help the lower jaw develop in a forward direction.

  • TAP 3

    Mandibular advancement device for the treatment of snoring and sleep apnea.

  • Fluoride Tray

    Two-piece dental arch-shaped devices used to deliver a fluoride treatment to teeth by putting gel or foam into the upper and lower tray.
