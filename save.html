Snap-in

Snap-in dentures are the most effective choice when it comes to stability. They are held securely in place by anchors onto the existing teeth.

What makes them unique is the locator attachments embedded within the tissue side of the denture.

Partial

Partial dentures are used when a patient still has some of his or her natural teeth, such as when one or more teeth remain in the upper and lower jaw.

There is a pink-colored base that is attached to a metal piece. These two pieces hold the denture in the mouth.

  • Most durable
  • Lightweight
  • Easy to remove and put on

Complete

Complete dentures replace all of a patient’s teeth. They sit on top of the gums, as opposed to dental bridges that are anchored to existing teeth.

Complete dentures are typically placed within 8-12 weeks after the teeth have been removed/extracted.

  • Perfect dental appearance
  • Customized
  • Durable
  • Lifetime adjustments
