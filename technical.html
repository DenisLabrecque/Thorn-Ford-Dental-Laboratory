We rely on Arnold Dental Supply, Washington owned and operated company that has been serving the dental community since 1903. Full-service dental supply and faithful supporters of the WSDLA

Arnold Dental Supply is a locally owned and operated company that has been serving the dental community since 1903. Arnold Dental offers a complete selection of equipment from manufacturers such as Adec, Belmont, Gendex, Kavo, Marus, Midmark and SciCan to name a few.

TFDL is an AvaDent partner, providing you the same product and price with local service. This system is particularly useful in the All-On-4 protocol.

AvaDent is not only an easier, faster and better approach to creating dentures, but it also provides more security to patients in the form of a permanent digital record of their prosthesis.

We produce ideal and cost effective crowns and abutments from Encode impressions, either physical or iTero digital.

We employ the latest in Nobel Biocare Abutment and bar design including the new angled screw channel (ASC) for zirconia abutments.
